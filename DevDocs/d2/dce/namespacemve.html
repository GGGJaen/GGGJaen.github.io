◆ ROT_HILBERT_TO_MORTON

constexpr int mve::ROT_HILBERT_TO_MORTON = 0
constexpr

ROT_HILBERT_TO_MORTON the rotation of the hilbert permutation to the left or right. Hilbert curves require a gray-code as a base pattern, but the order of the elements can be chosen at will.

Change this value to rotate to the right (if positive) or to the left (if negative). This constant exists purely for convenience, so that the user doesn't have to manually change RAW_HILBERT_TO_MORTON.
